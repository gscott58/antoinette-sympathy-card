Thoughts and Wishes to My Family . . .

Brothers, Sisters, Nieces, Nephews . . .

I am saddened by the loss of yet another young Watkins Family member!   My husband and me send our most sincere,  heartfelt,  sympathy, and love out to the family.  

First , it was "Little" Leo,  followed by "Little" John,  followed by "Big" Bobby,  followed by "Bang Bang",  and now "Little" Antoinette.

We all have the responsibility of teaching the generation below us the truth,   not leading them astray,  believing fast cash, drugs, alcohol, and gambling is the way.

Antoinette ran away looking for true love in all the wrong places.   She thought she found it in her brother, aunts, and uncles;  but somewhere in her search, she was betrayed.   I cannot imagine her last thoughts, but it pains me to see another young loved one fall victim to the streets.

My message to people in my generation is;  let's get on the same page.  Let's make clear to the generations below us . . . that yes, they have the right to do as they choose, but they cannot choose the consequences their choices bring upon them;  bad choices lead to death and destruction.

Stay in school, stand for something, be leaders, not followers, be something the next generation will be proud of.

My question to people in my generation is; "Have we been the watchmen and shepherds of our younger generations, as God anointed us to be (or have we failed them)?"  

 

Sister & Aunt,

Christine (a.k.a. Tina)
